The Past Maple Mountain High School

From 2022-2025, I attended Maple Mountain High School, located in Spanish Fork Utah. During my time there, some of my achievements included High Honors, succeeding in multiple AP and college level courses, and earning the Seal of Biliteracy in Spanish.

The Present Brigham Young University

Right now, I am a freshman at Brigham Young University. I am going to apply to the accounting program and hopefully begin this fall, and plan to pursue a master's degree in accounting. I am especially interested in tax accounting. I have loved my time at BYU so far, and hope to continue to develop my mind and my relationship with Jesus Christ during this time.

The Future Learning Forever

Even after my college education is over, I plan to continue to learn throughout the rest of my life. Henry Ford said “Anyone who stops learning is old, whether at twenty or eighty. Anyone who keeps learning stays young.” I believe that I can learn anything I put my mind to, whether it's new technologies in my career, hobbies in life, or how to raise children.
